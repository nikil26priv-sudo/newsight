About Us

Newsight is dedicated to making AI-based visual assistance accessible and affordable. Our mission is to design wearable tools that improve mobility, independence, and everyday functioning for people with visual impairments. Through modern AI vision models and user-centered design, we aim to build a next-generation assistive device for real-world use.
