Newsight

Student-led, non-profit project that aims to use AI-powered glasses to support and empower the vision-impaired community.

About Us

Newsight is a student-driven initiative dedicated to creating accessible, affordable, and effective tools for individuals with vision impairments. Our mission is to combine modern AI technologies with thoughtful design to improve independence and daily experiences for the visually impaired community. This includes helping them with reading texts, identifying objects, identifying obstacles and also finding items.

Newsight Glasses — Version 1

Version 1 of our Newsight glasses is currently in concept development. Our goals include:

• Real-time object recognition
• Text-to-speech reading
• Obstacle detection
• Item-locating assistance

As development progresses, we will share updates, prototypes, and demos to showcase the evolution of our technology.
