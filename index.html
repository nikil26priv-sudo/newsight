About Newsight

Newsight is dedicated to creating AI-powered assistive eyewear that is practical, accessible, and supportive for individuals with visual impairments. We combine contemporary vision models with human-centered design to enhance mobility, independence, and everyday experiences.

Newsight Glasses — Version 1 (GLV-024P)

Our first-generation Newsight glasses are built on the GLV-024P multifunctional smart eyewear platform. This base provides robust camera, audio, and connectivity features that Newsight extends with accessibility-focused software.

Hardware Foundations

  • 8MP wide-angle camera with 1080p video and stabilization
  • 4-microphone array with noise reduction for clear voice control
  • Dual stereo speakers for prompts, narration, and calls
  • AI large-model support for visual understanding
  • 32GB internal storage for photos and recordings
  • Wi-Fi 6 and fast data transfer options
  • All-day battery with glasses cell + charging case
  • Physical buttons for quick control and AI mode exit
  • Voice commands such as “Take a photo” or “Start video”

Newsight Software Layer

  • Obstacle detection with gentle audio cues
  • Real-time environment narration for orientation
  • Text-to-speech for signs, screens, and printed pages
  • Item-finding support (e.g., “Where are my keys?”)
  • Iterative features shaped by community testing
Roadmap

Project Roadmap

Newsight is growing from an early idea into a practical accessibility tool. Here’s the journey so far — and where we’re heading next.

Completed

  • Idea started – 2024: Newsight began as a student-led initiative to create affordable AI glasses for people with low vision.
  • Consulted specialists – 2024: Early discussions with accessibility experts and vision-impairment specialists validated the concept.
  • Research ongoing: Studying AI models, hardware constraints, and user needs to guide technical development.

In Progress & Next Steps

  • Software development: Building navigation assistance, object recognition, and text-to-speech on top of the GLV-024P platform.
  • Next milestone — Hardware model: Designing a dedicated Newsight frame with improved comfort, battery life, and integrated sensors.
